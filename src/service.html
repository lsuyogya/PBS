Assist-Personal Activities

We offer comprehensive support to help you find and maintain suitable accommodation. Our team assists with navigating tenancy agreements, securing appropriate housing, and ensuring your living arrangements meet your needs and preferences.

You can expect:

  • Personal hygiene, including showering, bathing, oral hygiene, dressing and grooming.
  • Assist with toileting, bladder and bowel management including menstrual care.
  • Assist with medication as per the direction.
  • Assist with feeding, and meal preparation and grocery shopping.
  • Arranging and attending medical appointments.
  • Assisting you to be active in the community and supporting you to arrange activities.
  • Coordination of other supports such as cleaning and laundry duties.
  • Assist Incidental household supports
