About PBS

At Passion Believe Support, we are committed to improving the lives of individuals by offering tailored care under the National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS). Located in Adeliade, Australia, our goal is to deliver exceptional support that empowers participants to lead independent, meaningful lives.

Whether you require help with everyday tasks, navigating life transitions, or engaging in community activities, our experienced team of healthcare professionals is committed to supporting you in achieving greater independence and improving your quality of life.

Our Mission and Vision

What We Stand For

Our mission at PBS is to empower individuals by offering tailored support that meets their unique needs and goals. We believe in fostering independence and enhancing the quality of life for our participants through compassionate and professional care.

Personalized Care

We take the time to understand your unique needs and preferences, ensuring you receive tailored support.

Experienced Team

Our skilled professionals are dedicated to providing high-quality care with empathy and respect.

Community Focus

We are committed to helping you build and maintain strong connections within your community.

Holistic Approach

Our services address various aspects of your life, promoting overall well-being and independence.
